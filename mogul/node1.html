1 Overview

MOGUL, the MOzart Global User Library, is a virtual library of packages for the Mozart system, created and maintained by the user community. You can browse its contents by visiting a MOGUL Archive, e.g.:

A MOGUL Archive is maintained automatically by a MOGUL Librarian.
